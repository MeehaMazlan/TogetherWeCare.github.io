Disabled People

The term "disabled people" refers to anyone who is unable to provide for himself or herself, entirely or partially, the necessities of a normal individual or social life due to a deficiency, congenital or not, in his or her physical or mental capabilities.

WHAT IS DISABILITIES

IMPAIRMENT

A person's physical structure or function, or mental functioning; examples of impairments include limb loss, vision loss, and memory loss.

LIMITATIONS OF ACTIVITIES

Difficult to do any activites such as seeing, hearing, walking, or problem solving.

PARTICIPATION RESTRICTIONS

Difficulties in daily activity such as working, engaging in social and recreational activites and obtaining medical attention and preventive services
